Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you need? Just send us a message above.

How do I book a parking slot?

You can book directly through our website or app. Select your preferred dates, choose a slot, and confirm payment. You'll receive a confirmation SMS and email.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes. Cancellations made 24 hours before arrival are fully refunded. Modifications can be made up to 6 hours before your scheduled time at no extra cost.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel), Visa, Mastercard, and bank transfers. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

Is there 24/7 security at the garage?

Yes. Our facility has round-the-clock CCTV surveillance, manned security personnel, and an automated entry/exit barrier system for every registered vehicle.

Do you offer monthly parking plans?

Absolutely. Monthly subscribers get a dedicated slot, priority support, and a 15% discount compared to daily rates. Contact us to get started.

Can I get a demo for my organization?

Yes — select "Request a demo" in the form above and we'll schedule a free walkthrough of the SmartGarage management system tailored to your team.
